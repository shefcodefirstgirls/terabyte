About Foodget It

The website created by students for students.

This website is designed as a hub for busy students who want to cook but can't find good recipes that are quick and cheap. We find and gather recipes that are simple enough to cook and wouldn't (and shouldn't) take up too much time. Each recipe listed has the cheapest possible price of each ingredient found in any supermarket. You don't need to spend tons to eat like royalty. You don't need to worry about it: we'll do it for you. Just forget about it. Just Foodget it.
